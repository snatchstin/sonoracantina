About Us

Welcome to Sonora Cantina!

Festive Mexican eatery serving breakfast, tortas & classic entrees in a colorful space. Established in 1990, Sonora Cantina has been serving Big Bear Lake residents for over 30 years now! We're on the boulevard, with outdoor and indoor seating available.

We are happy to answer all of your quetions by pohone during our business hours or through email after hours! Due to COVID-19 we are experiencing a high volume of calls. Please be patient and our staff will do their best to attend to every customer! Follow our socials for day to day updates!

To go orders can be made in person or over the phone, and we are currently not accepting reservations at this time. Thank you.
